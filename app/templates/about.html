About:

The Florida Commission on Human Relations handles discrimination complaints by individuals against their employers, their landlords, or against a "public accomodation" - a fancy way of saying "restaurant, hotel, motel,gasoline station, theater, or certain other public places or accommodations."

This site is not affiliated with the Florida Commission on Human Relations. This site was built to assist people in filing discrimination complaints, and in researching the results of past complaints.

Anything you read on this site isn't legal advice.
